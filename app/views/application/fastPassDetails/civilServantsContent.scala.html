Current civil servants

As a current civil servant or Fast Track applicant you're eligible for most of the schemes without a degree.

The schemes you'll need specific qualifications for are:

  • European
  • Government Economic Service
  • Government Operational Research Service
  • Government Social Research Service

Check the eligibility requirementsfor more information.

If you're currently a civil servant, or have previously worked for the Civil Service, make sure you read the conditions that apply.
